TFA

Melia has been accepted into the 2023 Teach For America corps. TFA is an organization that is focused on addressing educational inequities in underserved communities. Melia was selected from approximately 49,000 applicants nationwide to join the national teacher corps of recent college graduates and professionals who commit to teach in title 1 schools. Starting this summer, Melia will participate in an intensive summer training program to develop the skills and knowledge needed to achieve significant gains in student achievement, as well as engaging in professional development opportunities. Melia will be teaching middle school science at Governor's Village STEM Academy in Charlotte for the next 2 years! She is very excited for the opportunity to serve her community, working with Charlotte's youth.

Grad School

Following her time teaching, Melia plans to go back to school to continue her education. Melia is interested in becoming a mental health professional, working specifically with adolescents and teens. Continuing the journey in equity-work, Melia wants to work towards addressing accessibility and equity issues in the mental health field. Melia is considering programs for a Master's in Social Work, and a Master's in Therapy and Counseling as possible pathways. Eventually, Melia would like to attend a doctorate program in Psychology to become a Developmental or Clinical Psychologist.
